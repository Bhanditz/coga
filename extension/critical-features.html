SC Text: Critical features

Critical features and important information are above the fold and are accentuated in the primary modality of the content.

Suggestion for Priority Level (A/AA/AAA)

Related Glossary additions or changes

critical features
features that are required to complete the main tasks of the content
primary modality of the content
modalities considored in the design of the content
above the fold
the portion of a Web page that is visible in a browser window when the page first loads in the main modality of the content, that has been considored in the application design

Description

The intent of this Success cryteria is that users who do not remember to use the scroll will be able to use critical features.

When the main fetures are under the scrool bar these users will be unable to complet the main task. Thefor designers should iether have critical fetures above the fold or have a clearly labled mecanisim to help users find critical feturer.

This can also be achived via personlisation

Benefits

People with low execitive function, impared memory and other cognative and learning disabilities may not be able to find fetures that are under the scroll bar.

Many users are happy with important fetures being "discoverable" such that they had]ve to figuer out or learn how to use them. However people with some cognative and learning disabilities may lack the execitive function to figuer this out, and people with impared memory may not remember to use the scroll as a mechanisim for finding content.

 

For example , in an aplcation for drafting an email, the send button is a crtical feture without whoch the application has np us. Put any critical features, such as send, above the scroll.
