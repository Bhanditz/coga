W3C WAI Cognitive Accessibility Resources

Cognitive accessibility is highly personal and covers a wide range of requirements and barriers. The W3C WAI have developed several large, detailed resources which can be hard to navigate. This page is designed to provide with pointers to topics you should find interesting according to your interests.
